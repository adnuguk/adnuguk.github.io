Caledonian Code Crusade Featuring Mark Rendle – #ADNUGUK

Oct 10th, 2011 2:41 pm | Comments

Hot on the heels of speaking at DDDNorth Mark Rendle (@markrendle) starts the first leg of his “Caledonian Code Crusade” tonight in Glasgow at 1900. The agenda for this Crusade is as follows:

  • October 10th - Glasgow

  • October 11th - Edinburgh

  • October 12th - Dundee

  • October 13th - Aberdeen

More information about the above tour can be found from the man himself in his blog post.

This tour is gratefully sponsored by our friends over at Devexpress and Dot Net Solutions. Thank you very much for your help and support!
